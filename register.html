Register to the event on eventbrite

The Brainhack school is organized at four different institutions (see below). The course will start on August 5th at 9 am, and will run from Monday to Friday until August 30th. The school will be open each day from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., with a lunch break from 12 am to 1 pm. Registration is free for participants enrolled in one of the four partner institutions, and 50 CAD otherwise. Please contact Claude Godbout for any question.

Graduate students (Master and PhD) at the psychology department of Université de Montréal can register to brainhack school as a 3 credit course called "PSY 6983 - Projet en sciences des données neuronales" . Graduate students at École Polytechnique, Concordia University and McGill University can register to PSY 6983 through the Bureau de coopération inter-universitaire.

Week 1 at McGill University will be organized at the "visualization studio, room A" of the Redpath research library.The library is located at 3459 McTavish Street, Montreal, Quebec H3A 0C9.
Week 2 will be organized at the "Centre de recherche de l'institut de gériatrie universitaire de Montréal" (CRIUGM), wich is affiliated with Université de Montréal. The center is located at 4565, Chemin Queen-Mary, Montréal (Québec), H3W 1W5.
Week 3 will be organized at the École Polytechnique located at 2900 Edouard Montpetit Blvd, Montreal, QC H3T 1J4.
Week 4 will be organized at Concordia University, John Molson Building, 1600 Boulevard de Maisonneuve Ouest, Montréal, QC, room MB 2.430.
