brainhack week1

Project definition

Week 1 will introduce participants to a computational reproducibility toolkit, as well as a basic grounding in supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods. Short lectures and hands-on tutorials throughout the five days will provide participants with familiarity applying these methods to real data. Students will be paired with mentors at the end of Week 1 to provide more direct oversight of their projects. International guest residents will also be present for one week each during the school. As a participant, at the end of Week 1 you should be able to answer questions such as:

  • What is version control, and how can I use it to improve my workflow?
  • Which data standards can be used to organize neuroimaging data, and why should I adopt them?
  • How should I visualize and define features for machine learning in neuroimaging?
  • What are the basic principles underlying deep learning, and how do they differ from classical machine learning?
