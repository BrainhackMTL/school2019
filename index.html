Why BrainHack

Project-based, multidisciplinary learning

My advice on learning Python:

Don't set out to "learn Python". Choose a problem you're interested in and learn to solve it with Python.

— Jake VanderPlas (@jakevdp) September 10, 2017

Neuroimaging data requires both statistical and computational tools to be interact with neuroscience knowledge. These skills are much better acquired and solidified through practice. We call this process "brainhacking." Most of the brainhack training consists of working on a project openly and collaboratively.

Neuroimaging Data Science

The Brainhack School is a 4-weeks long full-time educational course at the interface of Neuroscience, Statistical modeling and Computer Science.

Not a bootcamp

An exploratory, collaborative, and educational course for neuroimaging data scientists

Participants have access to top work environment and computational facilities. Most importantly, the brainhack school community is here to help.
